THE HISTORY

WPA is the abbreviation of the Western Pack Alliance, created since 2016. For Werewolves, Weredog, Werefox and Werewolf Cybrog or Metal Wolf. Initial members consist of 11 Packs of Western Pack, namely:
  1. Western Pack 02
  2. Western Pack 03
  3. Western Pack 08
  4. Western Pack 10
  5. Western Pack 14
  6. Western Pack 23
  7. Western Pack 25
  8. Western Pack 38
  9. Western Pack 43
  10. Western Pack 56
  11. Western Pack 80

There are 32 members from the 11 Pack. The First Chief was Blizzard Wolf Smith. a Chief serves for 1 year in 1 term A few months later, several packs joined WPA, namely:

  1. Scoutra Pack
  2. Dracoa Pack
  3. Blue Core Pack
  4. Blood Moon Pack
  5. Shadows Lupine Pack
  6. Research Pack
  7. Dreadnought Pack
  8. Puppers Pack
  9. Moontoon Ore Pack
  10. Silvern Groundoned Pack
  11. Metallic Pack

After 6 years later in WPA participated in several events from outside the alliance and even events from members of their own pack.After that, several years later, WPA always received appreciation from all institutions, even humans wanted to join WPA. Now WPA is focusing on research on Military, Medical and Medicine Technology

WPA's home base in the Westernmost Section of Bridge Stone Forest, Bridge Stone National Park.
